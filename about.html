Everyone has a thing

Growing up, only a few things made sense to me without question: no ketchup on hotdogs, corn tortillas beat flour every time, and you never leave the house without a pencil and some paper.

That last one makes the most sense now.

Everyone has a “thing,” right?
Mine was filling sketchbooks—trying to redraw the pages and panels from my favorite comics, or making up new ones altogether.

I’m not a comic book artist… yet.
But it’s definitely next on my stop list.

I’ve spent a few years doing graphic design in the screen-print and embroidery world, and these days I co-run a small custom apparel and embroidery shop called Baby Dragon, while taking up the occasional odd job.

Now I’m stepping up my game and putting my sketchbook out there for you all to see—mistakes, experiments, half-baked ideas and all.

Welcome to Lilman’s sketchbook.
